The Product

An EpiPen,
redrawn.

We didn't shrink an EpiPen. We rebuilt every part inside , a new mechanism, new geometry, equivalent intramuscular dose , and integrated it into a phone case you'll actually carry.

Form 11.4 mm thin Same profile as a premium phone case.
Cartridge 0.3 mg epinephrine Therapeutically equivalent IM dose.
Release Two-step release Deliberate by design , secure in a pocket, fast in an emergency.
In the moment

Three steps.
Under ten seconds.

01

Pull the orange tab.

A deliberate two-step pull releases the auto-injector from the back of your case , secure enough that it won't deploy in a pocket or bag, quick enough to use in seconds.

02

Press firmly into the outer thigh.

A spring-loaded mechanism deploys the needle and delivers the intramuscular dose. You'll hear a click.

03

Hold for three seconds. Call 911.

Massage the injection area for ten seconds and seek emergency medical care , even if symptoms improve.
